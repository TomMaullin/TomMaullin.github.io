Teaching

This page features a list of classes I have taught, including materials for classes I organized. For materials from other classes, please reach out to the respective course organiser.

Previous Teaching

  • Graduate Teaching for Interdisciplinary Bioscience CDT programme
    Doctoral Training Centre · University of Oxford · Oxford, England, United Kingdom

    Since 2023, I have lead the 'Introduction to Machine Learning' and 'Statistical Methods' classes for the Interdisciplinary Bioscience CDT programme. Specifically, I design the curriculum for these short, intensive modules, prepare the course material and deliver the practicals and lectures.

    • Introduction to Machine Learning 2023. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Lectures] [Practicals]
    • Statistical Methods 2023. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Lectures] [Practicals]
  • Graduate Teaching for Health and Data Sciences CDT programme
    Big Data Institute · University of Oxford · Oxford, England, United Kingdom

    Since 2020, I have taught on the Health and Data Sciences CDT programme at the Big Data Institute, University of Oxford. In particular, I organize and run the 'Introduction to Python' and 'Imaging with Nilearn' intensive crash courses, mentor small teams of students in statistical analysis on the 'Data Challenge' course, and have previously assisted on the 'Computational Statistics' and 'Modern Statistical Methods' courses. A full list is given below, with material provided for the courses I have organized.

    • Imaging with Nilearn 2022. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Course Material]
    • Introduction to Python 2022. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Course Material]
    • Data Challenge 2022. Role: Statistics Mentor.
    • Imaging with Nilearn 2021. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Course Material]
    • Introduction to Python 2021. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Course Material]
    • Modern Statistical Methods 2021. Role: Assistant Tutor.
    • Data Challenge 2020. Role: Statistics Mentor.
    • Introduction to Python 2020. Role: Course Organizer and Tutor. [Course Material]
    • Computational Statistics 2020. Role: Assistant Tutor.

  • Tutoring Assistant for Interdisciplinary Bioscience CDT programme
    Doctoral Training Centre · University of Oxford · Oxford, England, United Kingdom

    Between 2019 and 2021, I regularly assisted on the Interdisciplinary Bioscience CDT programme for the Doctoral Training Center, University of Oxford. The classes were typically a couple of days long and served as introductory courses in which students learnt how to work with CNN's and regression modelling in R. Below is a list of classes I assisted on.

    • Statistics in Python 2021. Role: Assistant Tutor.
    • Machine Learning 2021. Role: Assistant Tutor.
    • Modern Statistical Methods 2020. Role: Assistant Tutor.
    • Machine Learning 2019. Role: Assistant Tutor.
    • Modern Statistical Methods 2019. Role: Assistant Tutor.
