About me

My name is Tom Maullin and I am a Post-doctorate researcher at the Big Data Institute in the University of Oxford. My areas of research interest include distributed machine learning, spatial statistics, linear mixed models, and random field theory.

My recent publications have focused on providing probabilistic bounds for random excursion sets, and new derivations for Mixed Model parameter estimation ([1], [2], [3]). My recent software projects include developing tools for conducting large-sample fMRI linear mixed model and linear model analyses, generating spatial confidence regions for fMRI conjunction inference, and contributing to several pre-existing fMRI software packages, such as the SwE and IBMA toolboxes (see here for further detail).
